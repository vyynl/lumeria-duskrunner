## Overview
The [[Duskrunners]] are an eclectic collection of adventurers that each have their own unique characteristics and identities to call their own - that means you have a lot of freedom in the kind of character that you create. I understand that that may sound awesome to some, and intimidating to others. Keep me and each other in loop in [Discord](https://discord.gg/j9PVAAff) and have fun taking your time, bouncing ideas off each-other, and building an adventuring party together!

Let me know of any ideas you have, and I'll work with you about getting them fit into the world!

>[!info] Party Background
>Our journey will start with our adventuring party tackling their first solo-mission as fully fledged Duskrunner Spears. For character creation, that means that we will be starting the game at ***level 1*** and as a party that ***knows each other prior to the start of session 1*** since they are all squad-mates that went through training together in the same recruiting class.
