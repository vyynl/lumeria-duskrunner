**Lumeria** is the name given to the known world and primary setting of current historical, political, and magical affairs. Once a sunlit land of sprawling kingdoms, thriving cultures, and divine presence, it is now a realm blanketed in permanent darkness following the apocalyptic event known as [[Nightfall]].

With the disappearance of the sun, most of the natural world collapsed. The survivors formed bastions of civilization around artificial sources of light — massive city-states known as #Holds, and the surrounding #Shards that exist in allegiance or dependency to them. These settlements represent the last outposts of organized life in a world now ruled by fear, scarcity, and shadow.

Lumeria is home to a wide variety of peoples, faiths, and philosophies — some clinging to remnants of the old world, others forging new identities in the dark. While much of the surface remains uncharted or overrun by [[Nyxwraiths]], organizations like the [[Duskrunners]] and other neutral actors work to maintain fragile threads of connection between Shards, Holds, and surviving factions.

Though memory of sunlight fades with each generation, the dream of a reclaimed dawn endures — whispered in prophecy, sought in academia, and carried by those who still dare to run the dark.
