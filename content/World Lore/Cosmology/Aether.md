## Overview

The Aether is the mirrored, coexistent plane of reality where **will**, **desire**, and **meaning** take form — a metaphysical structure shaped by expression. Every action, emotion, memory, or belief made real in the material world is reflected in the Aether and given shape.

It is the **source of all  magic** in Lumeria. When one casts a spell, invokes a god, or even acts with deep conviction, they shape the Aether. The gods themselves — [[Sael]], [[Nystra]], and [[Theryn]] — are not beings with autonomous will, but **massive, stable echoes** formed from the collective mortal resonance of will.

---
## Nature of the Aether

- **Mirrored Reality:** The Aether is layered over the material world like a reflection — it mirrors but does not mimic.
- **Shape by Will:** Desire and intention shape geography, creatures, and forces within the Aether.
- **Dual Function:** It serves both as a record of mortal expression and a catalyst for magic.

---
## Aetheric Phenomena

- **Echoes:** Residual impressions of intense experiences or decisions that may linger and repeat.
- **Resonance Storms:** Wild surges of aetheric instability caused by conflicting or overwhelming will-expressions.
- **Refraction Fields:** Regions where perception bends, often around forgotten ruins, dreams, or old battlefields.

---
## Relationship to the Divine

- **The Trinity:**
    - [[Sael]] – the expression of decisive action and the will to enact.
    - [[Nystra]] – the embodiment of perception, memory, and the unseen.
    - [[Theryn]] – the force of transformation, liminality, and contradiction.
- These gods are not external. They emerge from the aetheric accumulation of mortal expression.
- Divine manifestations (visions, powers, miracles) are echoes cast back by the pattern of one's interaction with the god’s domain.

---
## Cultural and Scholarly Understanding

- Scholars refer to it as **"The Mirror Plane"**, **"The Echo Fold"**, or **"The Living Grammar"**.
- In most traditions, the Aether is revered but not fully understood — its structure is too personal and vast to catalog fully.
- The [[Varnari Convergence]], an elite scholar sect, believes all gods are solvable patterns and seeks to map them completely.

---
## Player-Facing Truth

The Aether is not simply magic — it is **why** magic works. Every spell is an act of expression. Every divine act is a pattern recognized and returned. To interact with the Aether is to shape not just what happens, but **how meaning forms** in the world.
