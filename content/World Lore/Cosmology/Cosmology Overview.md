## The Nature of Magic
Magic in [[Lumeria]] isn't a formless gift, it's the act of shaping the world through *sheer force of will*. Every spell, ritual, or enchantment is an echo of the caster's **desire** drawn from their connection to the [[Aether]] — a mirrored plane of existence where intention has weight, and emotion has shape.

To cast is to harness the truth of your inner self and manifest it in the material. The stronger your **will**, the more vivid and powerful your magic.

> *"You do not memorize spells. You burn them into reality"*
>    Maurevel the Reflected
>     *Ten Thousand Faces in Still Water*

## The Aether
The [[Aether]] is a coexistent realm of reflected truth. All mortal expression is mirrored there — Cities of regret, storms of vengeance, rivers of longing — they exist in the aether as surely as earth and sky exist in the [[Material]].

> *"
>    Your desires take root in the Aether.
>    Your mind builds truth in the Aether.
>    The gods spring fruit in the Aether.
> "*
> Ferrun Kael-Azhur
> *The Folded Edge*

## The Threefold Mirror
There are three known gods in Lumeria — not beings with wills of their own, but ***manifestations of humanity’s most powerful expressions***. Mortals believe they answer prayer. In truth, they are what prayer *becomes*.

### **[[Sael]] — The Blade of Noon**
- **Domain**: Sun, Combat, Order, Flame, Oath
- She is the force of *action*. When you act with conviction, her light moves.
- Worship of [[Sael]] is rare now — she has grown distant since the Nightfall.

### **[[Nystra]] — The Mirror Beyond**
- **Domain**: Moon, Magic, Memory, Secrets, Dreams
- She governs the *inner world* — reflection, intuition, and arcane power.
- Most mages unconsciously walk her road, even if they don’t know her name.

### **[[Theryn]] — The Laughing Threshold**
- **Domain**: Dusk, Dawn, Change, Trickery, Death
- A shapeshifter. A psychopomp. The spark of transformation.
- [[Theryn]] is invoked in crossings — of lives, of truths, of selves.

The gods are not *good* or *evil*. Each contains both creation and destruction — light that warms and burns, dreams that heal and unmake, change that frees and destroys.

## Divine Aspects: Faces of the Gods in Magic
### **How Gods Take Shape**
The gods of Lumeria — [[Sael]], [[Nystra]], and [[Theryn]] — do not walk the world in flesh. They have no fixed form, no will of their own. Instead, they are *shaped into being* each time a mortal reaches into the [[Aether]] with intent.

Their **aspects** — personalities, images, or visions — are the reflection of the **caster’s own soul meeting the divine pattern**.

> *"When you channel [[Sael]], she is not speaking to you. You are speaking yourself in her shape."*
>    Sister Calbeth of the Wandering Light
>      *Hymns for the God Who Waits Outside Fire*

**What this means in practice:**
- Two clerics of [[Sael]] may see her differently: one as a golden-armored matron of justice, another as a burning-eyed child clutching a sword.
- [[Nystra]] may appear to a seer as a weeping moon-faced mask, while to a dream-thief she is a serpentine shadow of mirrors and smiles.
- [[Theryn]] might manifest as a fox-headed dancer to one caster, and as a flickering doorway made of laughter to another.

These forms aren't hallucinations — they are ***true manifestations*** of the god’s *aspect as filtered through individual desire*. They are real, they act, and they hold power. But they exist only as long as will sustains them.

### **Aspect Personalities**

While they vary from caster to caster, the **core pattern** of each god always carries familiar traits:

**[[Sael]] Aspects** *(Action Made Holy)*
- Speaks plainly, judges swiftly, burns righteously.
- May test your conviction or demand sacrifice.
- Her presence feels like a blade waiting to be drawn — warm, but never comfortable.

**[[Nystra]] Aspects** *(Truth Hidden in Shadow)*
- Speaks in symbols, dreams, riddles.
- May offer secrets or reflect your worst memory.
- Her presence often leaves behind something you didn’t know you’d lost.

**[[Theryn]] Aspects** *(Change That Knows Your Name)*
- Speaks in contradiction, laughter, or silence.
- May challenge your assumptions or wear *your face*.
- Their presence feels like a decision about to be made.
