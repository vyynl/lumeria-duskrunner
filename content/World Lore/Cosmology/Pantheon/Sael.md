## Overview

**Sael** is the divine manifestation of **will made action** — martial discipline, radiant conviction, and the burning pursuit of ideals. She embodies the sunlight not as illumination, but as **force**, **judgment**, and **self-directed power**.

Since the Nightfall and her metaphysical containment, Sael’s worship has diminished. Her echoes in the Aether are fractured but not extinguished. She now manifests only in those who act with unshakable clarity — or destructive fervor.

---
## Domain

- **Sunlight, Martial Action, Order, Oath, Fire, Sacrifice**

---
## Aspectual Duality

- **The Dawnbound Host** — honor, clarity, loyalty, purposeful action, bravery
- **The Pyre Unbound** — zealotry, self-annihilation, fanatical destruction, righteous terror

---
## Divine Expression

- **Clerics & Paladins of Sael** often feel her presence in the moment a decision is made.
- **Her Aspect** takes on the form that matches the caster’s conviction — a sword-wielding child, a matron of fire, or a faceless knight of ash.
- **Divine manifestations** of Sael are typically blinding, burning, or forcefully still.

---
## Post-Nightfall Worship

- Her faithful have split into two paths:
    - **The Ember-Kindled** – those who live quietly, acting as lights in the dark by example.
    - **The Pyrebound** – zealots who seek to reignite her power through dramatic sacrifice.
- Shrines are hidden. Worship is whispered. Symbols include:
    - A hand shielding a flame
    - A sun with broken rays

---
## Cultural Legacy

- Pre-Nightfall, Sael was the cornerstone of moral law and war oaths.
- Her temples served as courts, training grounds, and places of personal reckoning.
- Now, reverence of Sael is often associated with **fanaticism** or **foolish idealism**.

## Notes

- Sael’s metaphysical fracture caused the current imbalance in divine resonance.
- While her full echo is contained, fragments still surface in acts of valor, loyalty, and furious defiance.
- Siphoning has affected her domain deeply — though this is unknown to all but the innermost circles.

> *“Every oath is a spark. Every flame is a choice.”*
