## Overview

**Nystra** is the divine embodiment of the **internal world** — dreams, memory, perception, secrets, and the quiet weight of what is left unsaid. She is not darkness as absence, but as _presence_ — a fullness too vast to be seen directly.

Since the Nightfall, Nystra’s domain has flourished. With Sael fractured, the inner self has taken primacy over outward action. Her power is not in force, but in influence — **soft, endless, and patient.**

---
## Domain

- **Moon, Magic, Memory, Dreams, Secrets, Perception, Madness**

---
## Aspectual Duality

- **The Stillwater Veil** — reflection, foresight, healing through understanding, concealment as protection
- **The Maw of Echoes** — madness, obsessive memory, illusion, domination through hidden truth

---
## Divine Expression

- **Clerics and dreamers of Nystra** often encounter her as a mirror, a weeping mask, or a pale serpent.
- Her presence often brings **repetition, echoes, déjà vu**, or the unearthing of buried thoughts.
- She speaks **only in dreams**, symbols, and recursive truths.

---
## Current Worship

- Nystra is widely revered though rarely in formal temples.
- Her followers include:
    - **The Pale Archive** – memory-keepers who recover lost history through dream.
    - **The Masked Chorus** – performers who draw magic through reenactment of untold emotion.
    - **The Noctari** – lucid dream monks who sleep in staggered cycles to maintain psychic contact.

Symbols include:
- A closed eye with a tear
- A mirror that reflects stars but not faces
- A snake coiled into an infinity spiral

---
## Cultural Legacy

- Nystra has always been the patron of mages, archivists, and the grieving.
- Since the Nightfall, **all magic has tilted toward her domain**, and her influence shapes arcane practice, dreamscapes, and memory.

## Notes

- Nystra is not malevolent, but **uncaring in the way the sea is uncaring**.
- Her echoes thrive where memory is unstable and truth is buried.
- Some believe Sael’s silence is not just absence — but the result of **Nystra’s dream consuming her voice.**

> *“What you see is not what is. What is, you already know.”*
