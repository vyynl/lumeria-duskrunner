## Overview

The **Material Plane** is the physical world of Lumeria — the realm of form, history, and consequence. It is where mortals live, fight, build, and die. It is bounded by time, space, and substance, yet it is never truly separate from the metaphysical: the **Aether** overlays it at every moment, a silent mirror shaped by mortal will.

The Material is where desire is enacted. The Aether is where that enactment echoes.

---
## Core Traits

- **Tactile & Measured:** Governed by physics, time, and biology.
- **Mutable Through Will:** Mortal action shapes not only the world but its reflection in the Aether.
- **Bound to Mortality:** Death, decay, and limitation are fundamental properties.

---
## Post-Nightfall Condition

- Since [[Nightfall]], the Material Plane has been plunged into a state of **perpetual darkness**, as the metaphysical severing of [[Sael]]’s echo suppressed the daylight aspect of reality.
- **Time and seasons continue**, but sunrises no longer occur. Instead, scattered **pockets of artificial light** (magical, divine, or alchemical) maintain daylike conditions.
- The **absence of natural sunlight** has led to widespread cultural, magical, and ecological changes.

---
## Aetheric Influence

- Although the [[Aether]] overlays the Material, it is **not always aligned**. Certain places (ruins, battlefields, sacred groves) become **Aether-hot zones** where the boundary thins.
- All magic draws power through the caster’s [[Aetheric Connection]] — filtered by their will, and their training/patron.
- Echoes in the Material are rare but significant: phenomena where **aetheric events bleed visibly into physical space**.

---
## Geography & Ecosystems

- Many plants and animals have adapted to eternal twilight. **Bioluminescence**, **echo-sensitivity**, and **nocturnal evolution** are common.
- Certain regions are stabilized by ancient relics or surviving divine resonance — known as **Dayfast Enclaves**.
- The growth of **aetheric flora** and **arcane ecosystems** has blurred the line between magical and natural.

---
## Cultural Impact

- Faith traditions and civic calendars no longer reference the sun — instead, they use **local light cycles**, artificial dawns, or lunar rhythms.
- Many settlements are built around **light-ward cores** — magical beacons or divine sigils that prevent dream-bleed and maintain circadian health.
- The economy has shifted heavily toward **magical agriculture**, **aethercraft**, and **ritual labor**.

---
## Summary

The Material Plane is no longer “normal.” It is not just the stage upon which magic happens — it is the **consequence** of magic’s long echo. It is the plane of action, of cost, and of consequences that shape gods.
