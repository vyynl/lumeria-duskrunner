# The Weeping Wild
> *“To feel is to die. To forget is to survive.”*
## Overview
Before [[Nightfall]], the wood elves were children of the living world—rangers, herbalists, and guardians of the green. When the light vanished, they faced a terrible choice: *abandon the wilds*, or *abandon themselves*.

The result was a cultural rupture that still echoes through every wood elf heart. Those who chose the cities became mourners of a fading memory. Those who remained honed themselves into weapons—emotionless, lethal, and all but invisible to the [[Nyxwraiths]].

Today, they are collectively called the **Sylari**, but only the *Cityborn* still use the name freely. To the *Remnant*, there is no more need for names.

---
## Forest-Bound Sylari
Few speak of those who stayed. Fewer still return from meeting them.

These elves have trained themselves to the edge of mortality, severing emotion from their thoughts to burn so dimly that the Nyxwraiths pass them by. Their silence is perfect. Their movements, mechanical. Their souls, distant.

They do not hunt for glory or survival alone, but to preserve what is left of nature. To them, the cities are parasites, and the Sylari within them, already dead.

They take no names. They feel no joy. They live only so that the forest may endure another day.

---
## Cityborn Sylari
City Sylari live in a quiet, drifting sorrow. Cut off from the forests they once communed with, they have adapted to the tight walls and artificial suns of the Holds—but never fully. Their melancholy lingers like mist, felt even in silence.

Some work as scouts, herbalists, or guides. Others isolate entirely, struggling to find meaning in a world of stone. Most do not speak of the ones who stayed. When they do, it is with reverence, guilt, or deep unease.

To many, becoming a Duskrunner is a kind of self-exile—a way to escape the walls, to do something useful, or to punish themselves for leaving their kin behind.

---
## Public Perception
To outsiders, Sylari are a contradiction. Forest-bound elves are whispered about like ghosts—monsters in the woods who kill without a sound. Cityborn Sylari are seen as tragic, almost spectral people who walk through life with eyes fixed on something they can’t return to.

They rarely explain themselves. They don’t need to.

---
## Adventuring
- **Forest-bound Remnant**: Rarely become [[Duskrunners]] unless their training fails; if they do, it’s often out of exile. 
- **Cityborn**: May become [[Duskrunners]] for practical reasons, or out of longing to return to open skies, lost identity, or personal atonement.

The Sylari rarely seek glory. They walk to remember, or to forget.
