The Ashened Hand is a radical resistance movement rooted in the shard of Wellspring, operating in opposition to the Ironroot Corporation and its growing stranglehold over light, law, and livelihood. While they are branded as insurgents, arsonists, and kidnappers by the powerful, the Hand sees itself as the last living remnant of a purer flame—a force of cleansing, rebirth, and reckoning in a world of withered purpose.

The group’s methods range from sabotage and subversion to sudden, calculated strikes against Ironroot operations. Their cause, however, is not merely political. Beneath their coordinated actions lies a quiet, burning faith. A belief that something has gone fundamentally wrong with the world since **Nightfall**—and that only through fire can it be made right.

## Core Belief

At the center of the Ashened Hand's doctrine stands a figure cloaked in both myth and purpose: **Veylash**, called ***the Phoenix****.

Those within the Hand speak of her in hushed tones, reverently—rarely seen, always known. Some claim she was once a Duskrunner, others say a priestess or fallen noble. All agree on one thing: she burns with vision. To the Hand, Veylash is not a leader, but a spark. She is the fire passed from torch to torch—an embodiment of the change Sael demands, no matter the cost.

Members of the Hand follow her teachings more closely than any charter or code, and her words—when they arrive—spread like scripture through the ranks. Whether Veylash is prophet, martyr, or myth depends on who you ask. None who’ve met her seem eager to explain.

---
## Known Structure

The Ashened Hand maintains a network of decentralized **strike bands**, each operating semi-independently with purpose and fire. Leaders such as **Sergeant [[Toma]]** organize and execute missions at the shard-level, guided by the larger vision handed down through signs, whispers, or direct communion with Veylash herself.

Green-hooded cloaks mark members in the field, along with scorched sigils: an open palm wreathed in ash, pressed into armor and crates alike.

---
## Activities & Operations

- **Liberation of the magically inclined** from sectors like **Hearthgrove**—though whether these individuals go willingly is... disputed.
- **Targeted sabotage** of Ironroot caravans, equipment, and containment units
- **Excavation of forbidden ruins**, many believed sacred to Sael or tied to pre-Nightfall history
- **Recruitment through conviction**, often in low-light places where hope and hunger burn closest to the surface

___
## Public Perception

- **To Ironroot and its allies**: A dangerous, fanatical group threatening the fragile order of the shard.
- **To the desperate and dispossessed**: A glimmer of purpose in a dark world, where gods no longer answer.
- **To outsiders**: Mysterious. Frightening. Perhaps right. Perhaps mad.

Many fear what the Hand may be planning next.  
Some fear they have already begun.
