The **Bronze Gauntlet** is a loosely organized mercenary company operating in and around Wellspring. While not officially part of the Ironroot Corporation, their contracts—and loyalties—tend to align suspiciously well.

Recognized by their **green hooded cloaks** and tarnished brass insignia, the Gauntlet specializes in **enforcement, intimidation, and extraction**, particularly in districts where official oversight tends to “look the other way.”

They are not known for subtlety, but they are known for getting results. And for getting paid.

---
## Known Associations

- Frequently contracted by the [[Ironroot Corporation]] for off-the-record tasks
- Active in surveillance and pursuit operations in neighborhoods like **Hearthgrove**
- Generally avoided by locals unless hired or cornered

They are not well-liked.  
They don’t seem to mind.
